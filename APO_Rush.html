Requirements

Here is a little breakdown of our requirements for the rushing process and for active brothers. Donâ€™t worry, it is not a lot and there is absolutely no form of hazing. You will also have many friendly brothers to help you with the process.

As a Rushee:

  • Attend at least one info session
  • Attend at least one rush event

As a Pledge:

  • Attend the Pledge Ceremony
  • Attend weekly pledge classes to learn about the organization
  • Attend 2 fellowship events
  • Complete 10 hours of service
  • Plan a service and fellowship with your pledge class
  • Pass the final text with at least 80%
  • Attend the Initiation Ceremony
  • Pay pledge and chapter fees

As a Brother:

  • Attend 80% of weekly chapter meetings
  • Attend committee meetings
  • Attend any mandatory events
  • Pay quarterly chapter dues and annual national fees
  • Complete 10 hours of service
  • Attend 3 fellowship events
  • Complete 5 hours of fundraising
  • Attend at least 1 leadership event

Finishing all of these requirements becomes very easy when you are doing it with your friends! If you have any concerns at all about these requirements feel free to ask us and we will be glad to help you out. If you have any questions feel free to contact us!
